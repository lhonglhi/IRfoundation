About Intelligent Racing Foundation

The Intelligent Racing Foundation, a nonprofit organization based in Nevada, USA, is dedicated to advancing global AI innovation and STEM education. Our mission is to empower future innovators by providing world-class AI racing platforms, global standards, and open data access, fostering an ecosystem where technology and talent can thrive. We believe that true greatness lies in creating a ripple effect of innovation and opportunity that reaches millions.

To achieve this, the foundation collaborates with world-class experts to establish global standards for AI racing and data protocols, driving technological advancement and open collaboration. Through partnerships with leading research universities and scientific institutions worldwide, we have sponsored numerous AI autonomous racing competitions across the United States and Asia, providing a dynamic platform for education, research, and talent development. By pushing the boundaries of AI and fostering a global ecosystem of learning and innovation, the Intelligent Racing Foundation is shaping a future where technology serves as a catalyst for limitless possibility.

Education

STEM & Professional Education

Through strategic partnerships with governments, K-12 schools, and universities, the Foundation is transforming AI STEM education with a holistic "Race, Learn, and Play" approach. By integrating world-class AI competitions, cutting-edge AI curricula, and immersive AI Labs, we are empowering the next generation of innovators.

Our initiatives extend beyond the classroom — modernizing race tracks for AI-driven competition and developing interactive AI educational materials — bridging the gap between learning and real-world application to shape the future of intelligent mobility.

Our official AI Education Partner is Hitch Interactive.

Web3

Decentralizing AI Innovation

The Foundation believes decentralized blockchain and Web3 applications can democratize access to AI and emerging technologies, ensuring equitable global impact. By leveraging Web3's decentralized networks, we are building a borderless ecosystem that connects AI innovators, researchers, and industry leaders worldwide.

Beyond meetups, we host academic conferences, thought leadership forums, and global summits to drive collaboration and knowledge exchange. In partnership with Hitch Salon, we have gathered AI pioneers at Consensus @ Austin, CES @ Las Vegas, New York, Napa Valley, Milan, and Shanghai.

Through this decentralized model of influence, we are scaling AI innovation globally, fostering collaboration without borders, and accelerating the future of intelligent technology.
